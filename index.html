Our Story

Born from a Love of Pakistan

Founded in 2009 by a team of passionate Pakistani adventurers, Mega Tours began with a single mission: to share the extraordinary beauty of Pakistan with the world.

What started as a small trekking company in Gilgit has grown into Pakistan's most trusted travel brand, with offices in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and Gilgit. We've helped over 5,000 travelers discover the wonders of Pakistan — from the ancient ruins of Mohenjo-Daro to the icy peaks of the Karakoram.

Every tour we design is crafted with meticulous care, balancing adventure and comfort, local authenticity and international standards. We believe in responsible tourism that benefits local communities and preserves Pakistan's natural and cultural heritage for generations to come.

Common Questions

FAQs

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals as specified, ground transportation, professional guides, and park entry fees. Flights are included in select packages. Each tour page clearly outlines what's included and excluded.
Pakistan is a welcoming and largely safe destination for tourists, particularly in the northern areas and major cities. We continuously monitor security conditions and work with local authorities to ensure our travelers' safety. Our guides are trained in first aid and emergency protocols.
The ideal time depends on your destination. For northern areas (Hunza, Skardu), April–June and September–October offer the best weather. For cultural tours of Lahore and Islamabad, October–March is most comfortable. We offer year-round packages tailored to each season.
Most nationalities require a visa to visit Pakistan. Pakistan now offers e-visa for visitors from many countries, making the process straightforward. Our team can advise on visa requirements for your specific nationality and assist with documentation.
